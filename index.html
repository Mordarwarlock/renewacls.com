What is this website about?

Our site is designed to get you quickly up to speed on the current renewal options for the Advanced Cardiac Life Support test. Learn how each option works and what its advantages and disadvantages are, plus learn some tips to identify high quality and low quality course options.

Is This Site For Me?

If you are in need of some good information about renewal or recertification options for the ACLS, or if you haven't earned your initial certification yet, then you've come to the right place.

Online Vs Offline Renewal

The traditional Advanced Cardiac Life Support programs are notorious for being dry and sometimes difficult. Students have to plow through the latest American Heart Association guidelines or something similar and then listen to teachers.
